What is Center of Science and Technology Advanced Development?

CSTAD is a notable science and technology center in Cambodia. We guide Cambodian students in advanced science and technology, research, and digital skills development. Our graduates secure excellent job opportunities with top IT companies. CSTAD continues to provide high-quality training with the latest methodologies and is the best choice for those aspiring to become IT experts in Cambodia.
